The Cause

This tournament raises funds to support men's mental health initiatives — a cause deeply aligned with ATO's values of brotherhood, courage, and service.

We're partnering with the Movember Foundation to help change the face of men's health and fight to stop men from dying too young. By listening to our communities and reminding those close to us the importance of having the important conversations surrounding men's mental health, we hope to change behavior for the better and begin to take steps in the right direction.
